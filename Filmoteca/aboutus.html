About Filmoteca



Why did I choose to make an online library... of movies?


My idea of making an online library of movies was born from inconvenience. I hated the times I wanted to watch a movie and didn't know what to watch, especially when I started looking for the most watched movies or the most liked movies on the internet, because, often, they were not the same.


Therefore, with Filmoteca I wanted to bring as much information as possible about the movies which appeared in the last four decades, so that when someone wants to look for a film to watch, they will have all the information in the same place.
